Windy Days in Australia

This page contains two visualisations about windy days in Australia from 2007 to 2017.

Map of Windy Days in Australia

This map shows the number of windy days in each state for the year with the most windy days overall.

Side by side Bar Chart of Windy Days in Australia

This bar chart shows the trend of windy days in each state over the years.

Side-by-Side Bar Chart of Windy Days

This bar chart compares the monthly windy days across the top 3 states side-by-side. Use the year slider to explore different years and see which states and months have the most windy days.
